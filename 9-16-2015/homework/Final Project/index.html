    About BrineBuddy.com

    BrineBuddy is the internet’s leading source for pickle recipes, reviews, video tutorials, and more. Founded by a Brooklyn briner and fermenting enthusiast, BrineBuddy connects picklers and fellow canning comrades with specially-tailored ratings and reviews of pickle shops, our member’s beloved brands, pickle events, and local favorites from around the world.
